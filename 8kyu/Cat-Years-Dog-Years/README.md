# 🐱 Cat Years, Dog Years

## 📌 Difficulty

**8kyu**

---

## 📝 Problem

Given the number of human years, return an array containing:

```js
[humanYears, catYears, dogYears]
```

### Rules

### 🐱 Cat Years

- First year → 15 years
- Second year → +9 years
- Every year after that → +4 years

### 🐶 Dog Years

- First year → 15 years
- Second year → +9 years
- Every year after that → +5 years

---

## 💡 Approach

- Check whether the human age is **1**, **2**, or greater than **2**.
- Calculate cat and dog years according to the given rules.
- Return the result as an array.
